About KeePass-mww

KeePass-mww Viewer

This secure password viewer was created by Daniel Morvay of Morvay Web Works with assistance from IBM Bob.

This application is safe for all to use. Your KeePass database files are processed entirely in your browser - no data is ever sent to any server.

Privacy & Security:
• All processing happens locally in your browser
• No data is transmitted to any server
• Your passwords remain completely private
• Open source and transparent
